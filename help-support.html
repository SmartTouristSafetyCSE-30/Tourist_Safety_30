Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get a Digital Tourist ID?
You can register for a Digital Tourist ID at airports, hotels, check-posts, or online through our portal. You'll need to provide basic information and complete KYC verification.
What should I do in case of emergency?
Press the panic button in the app, call 112 for emergency services, or use the tourist helpline 1363. Your location will be automatically shared with authorities.
How does GPS tracking work?
GPS tracking is optional and helps ensure your safety. Your location is monitored for geo-fence violations and unusual patterns. You can disable it anytime.
Is my personal data safe?
Yes, all data is encrypted and follows Government of India data protection guidelines. Data is automatically deleted after your trip completion.
