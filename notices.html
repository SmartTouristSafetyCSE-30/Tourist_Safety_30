Official Notices

January 15, 2024
🆔 Digital Tourist ID Mandatory for All Visitors
All tourists visiting Northeast India must obtain a Digital Tourist ID for enhanced safety and security. Registration available at airports, hotels, and check-posts.
January 10, 2024
🚨 Emergency Response System Activated
24/7 emergency response system is now operational across all Northeast states. Tourists can use panic button feature for immediate assistance.
January 5, 2024
📱 Mobile App Launch
Smart Tourist Safety mobile application is now available for download. Features include GPS tracking, emergency alerts, and AI assistance.
December 28, 2023
🏥 Medical Emergency Network
Enhanced medical emergency network established across tourist destinations with helicopter ambulance services.
