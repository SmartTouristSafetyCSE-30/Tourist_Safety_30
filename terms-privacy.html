Terms of Service & Privacy Policy

Terms of Service

1. Acceptance of Terms
By using the Smart Tourist Safety System, you agree to comply with and be bound by these terms and conditions.
2. Service Description
This system provides digital tourist identification, GPS tracking, emergency response, and safety monitoring services for tourists visiting Northeast India.
3. User Responsibilities
  • Provide accurate and truthful information during registration
  • Keep your Digital Tourist ID secure and report any loss immediately
  • Use emergency services responsibly and only for genuine emergencies
  • Comply with local laws and regulations
4. System Availability
While we strive for 24/7 availability, the system may be temporarily unavailable for maintenance or due to technical issues.

Privacy Policy

1. Information Collection
We collect the following information:
  • Personal identification information (name, contact details, ID documents)
  • Location data (GPS coordinates, timestamps)
  • Device information (device type, operating system)
  • Usage data (app interactions, feature usage)
2. Information Use
Your information is used for:
  • Providing tourist safety and security services
  • Emergency response and incident management
  • System improvement and analytics
  • Compliance with legal requirements
3. Data Protection
  • All data is encrypted using industry-standard encryption
  • Access is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Data is stored on secure government servers
  • Regular security audits are conducted
4. Data Retention
Personal data is automatically deleted 30 days after trip completion, except for anonymized statistical data used for system improvement.
5. Your Rights
  • Access your personal data
  • Request data correction or deletion
  • Opt-out of non-essential data collection
  • File complaints with data protection authorities
